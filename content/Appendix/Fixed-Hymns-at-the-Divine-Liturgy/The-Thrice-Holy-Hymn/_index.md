#### Original

Ἅγιος ὁ Θεός, ἅγιος Ἰσχυρός, ἅγιος Ἀθάνατος, ἐλέησον ἡμᾶς.

#### English (Lash)

Holy God, Holy Strong, Holy Immortal, have mercy on us.

#### English (GOA)

Holy God, Holy Mighty, Holy Immortal, have mercy on us.
